The **Web**, also known as the **World Wide Web (WWW)**, is a system of interconnected web pages that can be accessed through the internet using a web browser. It allows people to find information, communicate, learn, shop, watch videos, and use online services from anywhere in the world. Websites are created using technologies such as **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript**, while servers store and deliver web pages to users. The web has transformed education, business, entertainment, and communication by making information easily available. Today, it is one of the most important technologies for connecting people and sharing knowledge globally.
